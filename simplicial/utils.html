medianshape.simplicial.utils.boundary_points(bbox)[source]¶

Returns corner points of a boundary box in which 4 corners of the bottom rectangle are the first 4 points, oriented CCW starting from (xmin, ymin, zmin). Similarly, 4 corners of the top rectangle are the last 4 points, oriented CCW starting from (xmin, ymin, zmax).

Returns all combinations of given values for n positions.
